Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my courses?

After logging in, navigate to the Dashboard where all your enrolled courses will be displayed. Click on any course to access materials.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click on the "Forgot password?" link on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link.

How do I update my profile information?

Go to Settings > Profile to update your personal information, change your password, or manage notification preferences.
